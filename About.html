Hi! I'm Peitao, a GIS Analyst, Structural Engineer, and BIM Modeler passionate about driving innovation in urban and architectural design. My focus is on integrating GIS, data analytics, and BIM to create sustainable cities and buildings that respond to today’s environmental challenges.
